## Description
Never miss your train again! NeverBeLate is the smart alarm app that knows when your train is running late—so you can sleep longer.

**HOW IT WORKS**
1. Set your destination and desired arrival time
2. Tell us how long you need to get ready
3. NeverBeLate finds the optimal train and sets your alarm

**SMART FEATURES**
• Live delay tracking from Deutsche Bahn
• Automatically picks the latest safe train
• Adjusts alarm when delays change overnight
• Native iOS alarm that works even on silent mode

**CUSTOMIZABLE**
• Set different commutes for each day
• Configurable safety buffer (conservative to risky)
• Multiple commutes per day supported

**PRIVACY FIRST**
• All data stored locally on your device
• No account required
• No tracking or ads

Perfect for commuters who want to maximize sleep without the stress of missing their train.

## Review Notes for Apple
This app uses:
- Background App Refresh to check for train delays
- Notifications to wake users
- AlarmKit (iOS 26+) for silent mode bypass
- Network access to query public train schedule API

The app does not require login or collect personal data.
